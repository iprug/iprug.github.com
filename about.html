Ipswich Ruby User Group is a group that meets to discuss the latest and greatest in the Ruby Community. That and we drink beer and like a good chat about all things technical.

We meet at the Brewery Tap on the first Tuesday of each month, at 7pm for a 7.30 start. There are usually two talks, or sometimes a bit of hands-on programming, followed by some nice geeky conversation at the bar. Everyone welcome, regardless of how much or little Ruby experience you may have (and whether or not you drink beer).

Contact Kerry for more information (and especially if you'd like to volunteer to give a talk).

Join the Google group to keep up-to-date.
